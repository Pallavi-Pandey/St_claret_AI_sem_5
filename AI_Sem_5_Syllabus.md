# Semester 5 - Artificial Intelligence (40 Hours)
## Course Syllabus & Time Distribution

This syllabus is designed to distribute the 12 major topics, 2 assignments, and 1 Mini Project evenly across exactly 40 hours of instruction and hands-on work.

### Part 1: Deep Learning Foundations (13 Hours)
* **Topic 1:** Intro to Neural Network & Deep Learning, Human Brain's functionality, Gradient Descent Optimization **(2 hours)**
* **Topic 2:** Neural Network Overview **(2 hours)**
* **Topic 3:** Neural Network Processing, Backward Propagation **(3 hours)**
* **Topic 4:** Train, Test & Validation Set, Vanishing/Exploding Gradient, Dropout, Regularization, Bias correction, Learning rate, Tuning, Softmax **(4 hours)**
* **Topic 5:** Optimizers: Adam, Ada, AdaBoost, RMS Prop etc. **(2 hours)**

### Assessment 1 (1 Hour)
* **Assignment 1:** Covering Topics 1 through 5. Administered via LMS platform. **(1 hour)**

---

### Part 2: Computer Vision (13 Hours)
* **Topic 6:** Computer Vision: What is CV? Reading Images in Python, OpenCV Intro, Basic Conversions, Resizing, Transformations, Contrast Stretching **(3 hours)**
* **Topic 7:** CNN Architecture: Convolution, Feature detector, Padding, Stride, Activation function, Pooling, Training CNN, Loss function **(4 hours)**
* **Topic 8:** Object detection concepts: Bounding box, object detection models, pre-trained models, transfer learning, segmentation **(3 hours)**
* **Topic 9:** Advanced CNN applications: Face detection, RCNN, fast RCNN, faster RCNN, mask RCNN, YOLO **(3 hours)**

---

### Part 3: Forecasting & Advanced NLP (10 Hours)
* **Topic 10:** Forecasting: Error metrics, Model Based Approaches, AR Model for errors, Data driven approaches, MA, ARIMA **(3 hours)**
* **Topic 11:** Forecasting Deep Learning: RNN architecture ideas, Vanishing/Exploding gradient, LSTM, GRU, RNN vs LSTM vs GRU, Training RNNs **(3 hours)**
* **Topic 12:** Advanced NLP models: Idea behind transformer, architecture and analysis, pretrainer models, Attention (Elmo, BERT, T5), Self-Attention, Transformer Block, MultiHead Attention, Encoder-Decoder Architecture **(4 hours)**

### Assessment 2 & Practical App (3 Hours)
* **Assignment 2:** Covering Topics 6 through 12. Administered via LMS platform. **(1 hour)**
* **Mini Project:** Practical hands-on project integrating concepts learned throughout the semester (e.g., combining CV or NLP models for a specific use case). **(2 hours)**

---
**Total Calculated Time:** 13 + 1 + 13 + 10 + 1 + 2 = **40 Hours**
